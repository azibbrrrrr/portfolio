## Introduction

- Led a team of 7 to develop a **meal-planning web app** with user
  authentication, fridge inventory tracking, and dynamic recipe recommendations
  using the **Spoonacular API**.
- Implemented **fridge inventory tracking** to help users manage their
  ingredients efficiently.
- Integrated **user authentication** for personalized experiences, allowing
  users to save preferences and access their meal plans.
- Provided **customized recipe suggestions** based on users' dietary
  restrictions and available ingredients.

## Features

### User Authentication & Profile Management

- Users can create and manage their profiles.
- **Profile editing** functionality allows users to update personal information,
  and modify dietary preferences.

  <Image
    src='/images/projects/shelflife/user-profile.jpg'
    alt='ShelfLife User Profile'
    width={600}
    height={100}
  />

### Dietary Restrictions Support

- Users can specify dietary restrictions such as vegetarian, vegan and lactose
  intolerance.
- Recipes are dynamically filtered to match users' dietary needs, ensuring they
  receive meal suggestions aligned with their health and lifestyle choices.

### Fridge Inventory Tracking

- Users can input and manage their available ingredients.
- The system automatically updates inventory after meal selection, reducing food
  waste.

  <Image
    src='/images/projects/shelflife/fridge.jpg'
    alt='ShelfLife Fridge'
    width={600}
    height={100}
  />

### Smart Recipe Recommendations

- Uses the **Spoonacular API** to fetch recipes based on available ingredients
  and dietary restrictions.
- Suggests alternative ingredients for unavailable items to expand meal variety.

  <Image
    src='/images/projects/shelflife/meal-a-day.jpg'
    alt='ShelfLife Meal A day'
    width={600}
    height={100}
  />

### Recipe Search & Ingredient Highlighting

- Users can **search for recipes** based on keywords and filters.
- When clicking on a recipe, the system highlights **ingredients the user
  already has** and **ingredients they are missing** from their fridge
  inventory.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS
- **Backend:** PHP
- **Database:** MySQL
